0,التفسِير: {وَلَقَدْ مَنَنَّا عَلَىٰ مُوسَىٰ وَهَارُونَ} اللام موطئة للقسم أي وعزتنا وجلالنا لقد أنعمنا على موسى وهارون بأنواع النعم والمنافع الدينية والدنيوية ومنها نعمة النبوة والرسالة {وَنَجَّيْنَاهُمَا وَقَوْمَهُمَا مِنَ ٱلْكَرْبِ ٱلْعَظِيمِ} أي ونجيناهما وقومهما - بني إسرائيل - من الغم والمكروه العظيم، وهو استعباد فرعون إياهم مع التعذيب بقتل الأبناء، واستحياء النساء {وَنَصَرْنَاهُمْ فَكَانُواْ هُمُ ٱلْغَٰلِبِينَ} الضمير يعود على موسى وهارون وبني إسرائيل أي ونصرناهم على أعدائهم - الأقباط - فكانوا الغالبين عليهم بعد أن كانوا تحت أيديهم مقهورين {وَآتَيْنَاهُمَا ٱلْكِتَابَ ٱلْمُسْتَبِينَ} أي أعطيناهما الكتاب البليغ في بيانه، الكامل في حدوده وأحكامه، وهو التوراة {وَهَدَيْنَاهُمَا ٱلصِّرَاطَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ} أي وهديناهما الطريق المستقيم الذي لا اعوجاج فيه قال الطبري: وهو الإِسلام دينُ الله الذي ابتعث به أنبياءه {وَتَرَكْنَا عَلَيْهِمَا فِي ٱلآخِرِينَ} أي تركنا عليهما الثناء الجميل، والذكر الحسن {سَلاَمٌ عَلَىٰ مُوسَىٰ وَهَارُونَ} أي سلام منا على موسى وهارون {إِنَّا كَذَلِكَ نَجْزِي ٱلْمُحْسِنِينَ * إِنَّهُمَا مِنْ عِبَادِنَا ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ} أي كذلك نفعل بمن أحسن وأخلص العبودية لله {وَإِنَّ إِلْيَاسَ لَمِنَ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ} أي وإِنَّ الياس - أحد أنبياء بني إسرائيل - لمن الرسل الكرام الذين أرسلتُهم لهداية الخلق قال أبو السعود: هو إلياس بن ياسين من سبط هارون أخي موسى {إِذْ قَالَ لِقَوْمِهِ أَلاَ تَتَّقُونَ} أي حين قال لقومه من بني إسرائيل ألا تخافون الله في عبادتكم غيره؟,"The interpretation: {And We had certainly bestowed favor upon Moses and Aaron} The lam is a preface to an oath, meaning, ""By Our might and majesty, We have bestowed favors upon Moses and Aaron of various kinds, religious and worldly, including the blessing of prophethood and messengership. {And We saved them and their people from the great distress} That is, We saved them and their people - the Children of Israel - from the great sorrow and harm, which was Pharaoh's enslavement of them along with the torture of killing the sons and sparing the women. {And We supported them, so they were the dominant ones} The pronoun refers to Moses, Aaron, and the Children of Israel, meaning, We supported them against their enemies - the Egyptians - so they became dominant over them after being under their control. {And We gave them the clear Book} That is, We gave them the book that is eloquent in its statement, complete in its boundaries and rulings, which is the Torah. {And We guided them to the straight path} That is, We guided them to the straight path that has no deviation. Al-Tabari said: It is Islam, the religion of Allah with which He sent His prophets. {And We left for them among the later generations} That is, We left for them beautiful praise and good mention. {Peace be upon Moses and Aaron} That is, peace from Us upon Moses and Aaron. {Indeed, thus do We reward the doers of good, [who were] of Our believing servants} That is, thus do We do to those who do good and sincerely worship Allah. {And indeed, Elijah was of the messengers} That is, Elijah - one of the prophets of the Children of Israel - was among the noble messengers whom I sent to guide creation. Abu Al-Saud said: He is Elijah, son of Yasin, from the tribe of Aaron, the brother of Moses. {When he said to his people, ""Do you not fear [Allah]?""} That is, when he said to his people from the Children of Israel, ""Do you not fear Allah in worshipping other than Him?"""
1,"{فَكَذَّبُوهُ فَإِنَّهُمْ لَمُحْضَرُونَ} أي فكذبوا نبيَّهم فإِنهم لمحضرون في العذاب {إِلاَّ عِبَادَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلْمُخْلَصِينَ} أي لكنْ عباد الله المؤمنين فإِنهم نجوا من العذاب {وَتَرَكْنَا عَلَيْهِ فِي ٱلآخِرِينَ} أي تركنا على إلياس الثناء الحسن الجميل إلى يوم الدين {سَلاَمٌ عَلَىٰ إِلْ يَاسِينَ} أي سلام منا عليه وعلى آل ياسين قال المفسرون: المراد بـ {إل ياسين} هو إلياس ومن آمن معه جمعوا معه تغليباً كما قالوا للمهلَّب وقومه المهلَّبون، واختار الطبري أنه اسم لإِلياس فيقال: إلياس، وإل ياسين مثل ميكال وميكائيل، وأن له اسمين فيسمى \"" إلياس \"" و {إل ياسين} {إِنَّا كَذَلِكَ نَجْزِي ٱلْمُحْسِنِينَ * إِنَّهُ مِنْ عِبَادِنَا ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ} تقدم تفسيره، وإِنما ختم الآيات بعد ذكر كل رسول بالسلام عليه، وبهاتين الآيتين الكريمتين لبيان فضل الإِحسان والإِيمان، وأن هؤلاء الرسل الكرام كانوا جميعاً من المتصفين بهذه الصفات، فلذلك استحقوا التحية والسلام، والذكر الحسن بين الأنام، صلوات الله وسلامه عليهم أجمعين {وَإِنَّ لُوطاً لَّمِنَ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ} أي وإِنَّ لوطاً لأحد رسلنا لهداية قومه {إِذْ نَجَّيْنَاهُ وَأَهْلَهُ أَجْمَعِينَ} أي اذكر حين خلصناه من العذاب هو ومن آمن معه من أهله وأولاده {إِلاَّ عَجُوزاً فِي ٱلْغَابِرِينَ} أي إلا امرأته الكافرة فإِنها لم تؤمن فكانت من الباقين في العذاب ومن الهالكين {ثُمَّ دَمَّرْنَا ٱلآخَرِينَ} أي ثم أهلكنا المكذبين من قومه أشدَّ إهلاك وأفظعه، وذلك بقلب قراهم حيث جعلنا عاليها سافلها وأمطرنا عليهم حجارة من سجيل، ولهذا عبَّر بـ {دَمَّرْنَا} {وَإِنَّكُمْ لَّتَمُرُّونَ عَلَيْهِمْ مُّصْبِحِينَ * وَبِٱلَّيلِ} أي وإِنكم يا أهل مكة لتمرون على منازلهم في أسفاركم وتشاهدون آثار هلاكهم صباحاً ومساءً، وليلاً ونهاراً {أَفَلاَ تَعْقِلُونَ}؟","So they denied him, and indeed, they are to be brought [to punishment]. Indeed, it is thus that We reward the doers of good. Indeed, he was of Our believing servants. And indeed, Lot was among the messengers. [And remember] when We saved him and his family, all except for an old woman among those who remained. Then We destroyed the others. And indeed, you pass by them in the morning, And at night. Then will you not use your reason?"
2,"{وَإِنَّ يُونُسَ لَمِنَ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ} أي وإِن يونس لأحد رسلنا المرسلين لهداية قومه {إِذْ أَبَقَ إِلَى ٱلْفُلْكِ ٱلْمَشْحُونِ} أي اذكر حين هرب إلى السفينة المملوءة بالرجال {فَسَاهَمَ فَكَانَ مِنَ ٱلْمُدْحَضِينَ} أي فقارع أهل السفينة فكان من المغلوبين بالقرعة فألقوه في البحر قال المفسرون: إن يونس ضاق صدراً بتكذيب قومه، فأنذرهم بعذاب قريب، وغادرهم مغضباً لأنهم كذبوه، فقاده الغضب إلى شاطىء البحر حيث ركب سفينة مشحونة، فناوأتها الرياح والأمواج، فقال الملاحون: هٰهنا عبدٌ أبق من سيده، ولا بدَّ لنجاة السفينة من إلقائه في الماء لتنجو من الغرق، فاقترعوا فخرجت القُرعة على يونس فألقوه في البحر {فَٱلْتَقَمَهُ ٱلْحُوتُ وَهُوَ مُلِيمٌ} أي فابتلعه الحوت وهو آتٍ بما يُلام عليه من تخليه عن المهمة التي أرسله الله بها، وترك قومه مغاضباً لهم، وخروجه بغير إِذنٍ من ربه {فَلَوْلاَ أَنَّهُ كَانَ مِنَ ٱلْمُسَبِّحِينَ} أي لولا أنه كان من الذاكرين الله كثيراً في حياته {لَلَبِثَ فِي بَطْنِهِ إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ يُبْعَثُونَ} أي لبقي في بطن الحوت إلى يوم القيامة، وأصبح بطنه قبراً له فلم ينج أبداً، ولكنه سبَّح اللهَ واستغفره وناداه وهو في بطن الحوت بقوله {لاَّ إِلَـٰهَ إِلاَّ أَنتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنتُ مِنَ ٱلظَّالِمِينَ} [الأنبياء: 87] فاستجاب الله تضرعه ونداءه {فَنَبَذْنَاهُ بِٱلْعَرَآءِ وَهُوَ سَقِيمٌ} أي فألقيناه من بطن الحوت على الساحل، بالأرض الفضاء التي لا شجر فيها ولا ظل، وهو سقيم مريض مما ناله من الكرب قال عطاء: أوحى الله تعالى إلى الحوت إني قد جعلت بطنك له سجناً، ولم أجعله لك طعاماً، فلذلك بقي سالماً لم يتغير منه شيء {وَأَنبَتْنَا عَلَيْهِ شَجَرَةً مِّن يَقْطِينٍ} أي وأنبتنا فوقه شجرة لتظله وتقيه حرَّ الشمس، وهي شجرة القرع ذات الأوراق العريضة قال ابن جزي: وإِنما خصَّ القرع بالذكر لأنه يجمع كبر الورق، وبرد الظل، والذبابُ لا يقربه، فإِن لحم يونس لما خرج من البحر كان لا يحتمل الذباب، وكان هذا من تدبير الله ولطفه، فلما استكمل قوته وعافيته ردَّه الله إلى قومه ولهذا قال {وَأَرْسَلْنَاهُ إِلَىٰ مِئَةِ أَلْفٍ أَوْ يَزِيدُونَ} أي وأرسلناه بعد ذلك إلى قومه الذين هرب منهم وهم مائة ألفٍ بل يزيدون قال المفسرون: كانوا مائة وعشرين ألفاً وقيل: وسبعين ألفاً، وهم أهل نينوى بجهة الموصل، و \"" أو \"" بمعنى بل أي بل يزيدون {فَآمَنُواْ فَمَتَّعْنَاهُمْ إِلَىٰ حِينٍ} أي فآمنوا بعد أن شاهدوا أمارات العذاب الذي وُعدوا به فأبقيناهم ممتعين في الدنيا إلى حين انقضاء آجالهم قال في التسهيل: روي أنهم خرجوا بالأطفال وأولاد البهائم، وفرقوا بينهم وبين الأمهات، وناحوا وتضرعوا إلى الله، فرفع الله العذاب عنهم … ولما انتهى من الحديث عن الرسل الكرام رجع إلى الحديث عن المكذبين من كفار مكة فقال {فَٱسْتَفْتِهِمْ أَلِرَبِّكَ ٱلْبَنَاتُ وَلَهُمُ ٱلْبَنُونَ}؟","""And indeed, Yunus was one of the messengers. When he fled to the laden ship. And he drew lots, and he was among the losers. Then a great fish swallowed him, and he was blameworthy. And had he not been of those who praise, he would have remained in its belly until the Day they are raised. So We cast him upon the open plain, and he was sick. And We caused to grow over him a plant of gourd, and We sent him to a hundred thousand or more. And they believed, so We gave them enjoyment for a time. Then ask them, 'Is it your Lord who has daughters and they have the sons?'"
3,"والغرضُ تعجيزهم وبيان أنهم لا يستندون - في أقوالهم الباطلة - على دليل شرعي، ولا منطق عقلي … وينتقل إلى أسطورةٍ أُخرى لفَّقها المشركون، حيث زعموا أن هناك صلة بين الله سبحانه وبين الجنِّ، وأنه من {سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَىٰ عَمَّا يَقُولُونَ عُلُوّاً كَبِيراً} [الإسراء: 43] ثم زعموا أن الملائكة إناث، وأنهن بنات الله {وَلَقَدْ عَلِمَتِ ٱلجِنَّةُ إِنَّهُمْ لَمُحْضَرُونَ} أي لقد علمت الشياطين أنهم أنفسهم بالعبودية والطاعة لله، والتنزيه له جل وعلا {وَإِن كَانُواْ لَيَقُولُونَ * لَوْ أَنَّ عِندَنَا ذِكْراً مِّنَ ٱلأَوَّلِينَ * لَكُنَّا عِبَادَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلْمُخْلَصِينَ} الضمير لكفار قريش و {وَإِن} يَسْتَعْجِلُونَ}؟","The purpose is to show their inability and to demonstrate that they do not rely on any legitimate legal evidence or rational logic in their false statements. He then moves on to another myth fabricated by the polytheists, claiming that there is a connection between God Almighty and the jinn, and that from the union between God Almighty and the jinn, the angels were born. He says, ""And they have made between Him and the jinn a kindred"" (Al-Israa: 43), meaning the polytheists made a kinship and lineage between God and the jinn, saying that He married from the jinn and the angels were born to Him. ""Glorified is He and Exalted above what they say, a great Exaltation"" (Al-Israa: 43). Then they claimed that the angels are females and that they are the daughters of God. ""And indeed, the jinn have known that they will be brought forth"" (Al-Saffat: 158), meaning the devils have known that they will be brought forth to punishment. Al-Sawi said: This is an increase in their humiliation and lying, as if it were said: Those whom you have exalted and made the daughters of God, know your condition and what your affair will end with. ""Glorified is Allah of what they attribute"" (Al-Saffat: 159), meaning Allah is free and exalted from what these wrongdoers attribute to Him. ""Except the devoted slaves of Allah"" (Al-Saffat: 160), an interrupted exception, meaning but the devoted slaves of Allah, for they glorify Allah from what these attribute to Him. ""Then indeed, you and what you worship, you are not causing anyone to stray, except whoever is destined for the Hellfire"" (Al-Saffat: 161-163), meaning, O disbelievers, you and all that you worship of idols and devils are not able to misguide anyone from the slaves of Allah, except the one whom Allah has decreed wretchedness for, and has destined to enter the Fire and burn in it. Then Allah mentioned the angels' acknowledgment of servitude to Allah, saying, ""And there is not one of us except he has a known station"" (Al-Saffat: 164), meaning, there is not an angel among us except he has a rank, position, and duty that he does not transgress. Some of us are entrusted with sustenance, some with lifespans, some descend with revelation, and each has his station of worship, proximity, and honor. ""And indeed, we are those who stand in rows"" (Al-Saffat: 165), meaning, those who stand in worship in rows. ""And indeed, we are those who glorify"" (Al-Saffat: 166), meaning, those who glorify Allah from everything that is not befitting His Majesty and Greatness, we glorify Allah at all times. In Al-Tasheel, it is said: In this speech of the angels, there is a refutation of those who said they are the daughters of God and the partners of God, because they acknowledged their servitude, obedience, and glorification of Allah, the Exalted. ""And they used to say, 'If only we had a reminder from the former peoples, we would have been the devoted slaves of Allah'"" (Al-Zukhruf: 44-45), the pronoun refers to the disbelievers of Quraysh, and ""And"" is the lightened form of the heavy ""Inn,"" meaning, and if the case and affair were that the disbelievers of Makkah used to say before the Qur'an was revealed to them, if a book from the books of the former peoples, like the Torah and the Gospel, had been revealed to us, we would have been greater in faith than them, and more in worship and devotion to Allah than them. But when the Qur'an came to them, they disbelieved in it. Therefore, He said, ""But they disbelieved in it"" (Al-Zukhruf: 46), meaning, they disbelieved and denied the Qur'an, the most noble of the heavenly books. ""Then they will soon know"" (Al-Zukhruf: 47), meaning, they will soon see the consequence of their disbelief in the verses of Allah, and it is a threat and warning. ""And indeed, Our word has preceded for Our slaves, the messengers"" (Al-Saffat: 171), meaning, Our promise and decree for the noble messengers have preceded. ""Indeed, they are the ones who will be victorious"" (Al-Saffat: 172), meaning, they are the ones who will be victorious over their enemies, and the reference is to Allah's saying, ""Allah has decreed, 'I will surely prevail, I and My messengers'"" (Al-Mujadila: 21). ""And indeed, Our soldiers are the ones who will be victorious"" (Al-Saffat: 173), meaning, and indeed, Our soldiers, the believers, are the ones who will be victorious in this world and the Hereafter, in this world with proof and argument, and in the Hereafter with entering Paradise. The interpreters said: Allah's victory for the believers is certain, and their defeat in some battles does not negate this, for the rule is with victory and triumph, and they are only defeated sometimes due to their negligence or as a trial and affliction. ""Then turn away from them for a time"" (Al-Dhariyat: 54), meaning, turn away from them, O Muhammad, for a short time, until you are commanded to fight them. ""And see them, then they will soon see"" (Al-Dhariyat: 55), meaning, and see them when punishment befalls them, then they will soon see the consequence of their disbelief. ""Do they then hasten on with Our punishment?"" (Al-Naml: 72)."

يمنع بعضكم بعضاً ويقال إنهم مسؤولون عن تركهم لا إله إلا الله {بَلْ هُمُ ٱلْيَوْمَ} وهو يوم القيامة {مُسْتَسْلِمُونَ} استسلم العابد والمعبود لله وعلموا أن الحق لله {وَأَقْبَلَ بَعْضُهُمْ عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍ} الإنس على الشياطين والسفلة على القادة {يَتَسَآءَلُونَ} يتلاومون ويتخاصمون {قَالُوۤاْ} يعني الإنس للشياطين {إِنَّكُمْ كُنتُمْ تَأْتُونَنَا عَنِ ٱلْيَمِينِ} تغووننا عن الدين {قَالُواْ} يعني الشياطين للإنس {بَلْ لَّمْ تَكُونُواْ مُؤْمِنِينَ} بالله {وَمَا كَانَ لَنَا عَلَيْكُمْ مِّن سُلْطَانٍ} من عذر وحجة نأخذكم بها {بَلْ كُنتُمْ قَوْماً طَاغِينَ} كافرين بالله {فَحَقَّ عَلَيْنَا} فوجب علينا {قَوْلُ رَبِّنَآ} بالسخط والعذاب {إِنَّا لَذَآئِقُونَ} العذاب في النار {فَأَغْوَيْنَاكُمْ} أضللناكم عن الدين {إِنَّا كُنَّا غَاوِينَ} ضالين عن الدين {فَإِنَّهُمْ يَوْمَئِذٍ} يوم القيامة {فِي ٱلْعَذَابِ مُشْتَرِكُونَ} العابد والمعبود {إِنَّا كَذَٰلِكَ} هكذا {نَفْعَلُ بِٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ} المشركين {إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوۤاْ إِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ} في الدنيا قولوا {لاَ إِلَـٰهَ إِلاَّ ٱللَّهُ يَسْتَكْبِرُونَ} يتعاظمون عن ذلك {وَيَقُولُونَ أَئِنَّا لَتَارِكُوۤاْ آلِهَتِنَا} عبادة آلهتنا {لِشَاعِرٍ مَّجْنُونٍ} يختلق يعنون محمداً صلى الله عليه وسلم {بَلْ جَآءَ} محمد عليه السلام {بِٱلْحَقِّ} بالقرآن والتوحيد {وَصَدَّقَ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ} وبتصديق المرسلين قبله {إِنَّكُمْ} يا أهل مكة {لَذَآئِقُو ٱلْعَذَابِ ٱلأَلِيمِ} الوجيع في النار {وَمَا تُجْزَوْنَ} في الآخرة {إِلاَّ مَا كُنتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ} في الدنيا في الكفر والشرك {إِلاَّ عِبَادَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلْمُخْلَصِينَ} المعصومين من الكفر والشرك ويقال المخلصين بالعبادة والتوحيد إن قرأت بخفض اللام {أُوْلَـٰئِكَ لَهُمْ رِزْقٌ مَّعْلُومٌ} طعام معروف على قدر غدوة وعشية في الدنيا وليس ثم بكرة ولا عشية {فَوَاكِهُ} لهم ألوان الفواكه {وَهُم مُّكْرَمُونَ} بالتحف {فِي جَنَّاتِ ٱلنَّعِيمِ} لا يفنى نعيمها {عَلَىٰ سُرُرٍ مُّتَقَابِلِينَ} متواجهين في الزيارة {يُطَافُ عَلَيْهِمْ} في الخدمة {بِكَأْسٍ} بخمر {مِّن مَّعِينٍ} من خمرة طاهرة.,"God says to the angels, ""Gather those who did wrong"" - those who associated partners with Him, ""and their companions"" - their mates and partners from among the jinn, humans, and devils, ""and what they used to worship besides Allah"" - from idols. ""Then lead them"" - take them ""to the path of Hell"" - to the middle of the fire. God says to the angels, ""Stop them"" - hold them back at the fire, ""for they are to be questioned"" - about this matter. ""What is the matter with you that you do not help each other?"" - you do not prevent one another from Allah's punishment. It is said that they will be questioned about their neglect of saying, ""There is no god but Allah."" ""But they, this Day"" - the Day of Judgment, ""are submissive"" - the worshipper and the worshipped have submitted to Allah and have known that the truth belongs to Allah. ""And some of them will turn to others"" - humans to devils and the lowly to the leaders, ""questioning each other"" - blaming and quarreling. They will say - the humans to the devils, ""You used to come to us from the right"" - you used to mislead us from the religion. They will say - the devils to the humans, ""Nay, you yourselves were not believers"" - in Allah. ""And we had no authority over you"" - no excuse or argument to take you by. ""Nay, you yourselves were a transgressing people"" - disbelievers in Allah. ""So the word of our Lord has proved true against us"" - the word of our Lord has been fulfilled upon us, ""that we shall indeed (all) taste (the punishment)"" - the punishment in the Fire. ""And we led you astray"" - we misguided you from the religion, ""for we were (ourselves) astray"" - misguided from the religion. ""So verily, on that Day, they will be together in the punishment"" - the worshipper and the worshipped. ""Thus do We deal with the Mujrimun (criminals, disbelievers, polytheists, sinners)"" - the polytheists. ""Verily, when it was said to them: 'La ilaha illa Allah (none has the right to be worshipped but Allah),' they puffed themselves up with pride (i.e. they rejected it)"" - they were too proud to accept it. ""And they said: 'Shall we leave our gods for the sake of a mad poet (Muhammad)?"" - who fabricates, meaning Muhammad, peace be upon him. ""Nay, he has brought the truth"" - the Quran and monotheism, ""and he has confirmed the Messengers (before him)"" - and he has confirmed the Messengers before him. ""Verily, you (O people of Makkah) will taste the painful punishment"" - the severe punishment in the Fire. ""And you will not be recompensed except for what you used to do"" - in the world in disbelief and polytheism. ""Except those who are the slaves of Allah (faithful and obedient to Allah)"" - those who are protected from disbelief and polytheism. It is said, those who are sincere in worship and monotheism, if you read with the lowering of the lam. ""Those, for them is a known provision"" - known food, as much as they need in the morning and evening in the world, and there is no morning or evening there. ""Fruits"" - they will have various kinds of fruits, ""and they will be honored"" - with gifts, ""in the Gardens of Bliss"" - whose bliss will never end. ""On thrones facing one another"" - facing each other in visits. ""A cup will be passed round among them"" - in service, ""of clear flowing wine"" - from pure wine."
7,ثم أخبر الله سبحانه عن الأتباع، والمتبوعين بقوله {فَإِنَّهُمْ يَوْمَئِذٍ فِى ٱلْعَذَابِ مُشْتَرِكُونَ} كما كانوا مشتركين في الغواية {إِنَّا كَذَلِكَ نَفْعَلُ بِٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ} أي إنا نفعل مثل ذلك الفعل بالمجرمين، أي أهل الإجرام، وهم المشركون كما يفيده قوله سبحانه {إِنَّهُمْ كَانُواْ إِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ لاَ إِلَـٰهَ إِلاَّ ٱللَّهُ يَسْتَكْبِرُونَ} أي إذا قيل لهم قولوا لا إلٰه إلاّ الله يستكبرون عن القبول، ومحل يستكبرون النصب على أنه خبر كان، أو الرفع على أنه خبر إن، وكان ملغاة {وَيَقُولُونَ أَإِنَّا لَتَارِكُو ءالِهَتِنَا لِشَاعِرٍ مَّجْنُونٍ} يعنون النبي صلى الله عليه وسلم، أي لقول شاعر مجنون، فردّ الله سبحانه عليهم بقوله {بَلْ جَاء بِٱلْحَقّ} يعني القرآن المشتمل على التوحيد، والوعد، والوعيد {وَصَدَّقَ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ} أي صدّقهم فيما جاءوا به من التوحيد، والوعيد، وإثبات الدار الآخرة، ولم يخالفهم، ولا جاء بشيء لم تأت به الرسل قبله {إِنَّكُمْ لَذَآئِقُو ٱلْعَذَابَ ٱلأَلِيمَ} أي إنكم بسبب شرككم، وتكذيبكم لذائقوا العذاب الشديد الألم.,"Then Allah, the Exalted, spoke about the followers and the leaders, saying, ""Indeed, they will be in the punishment [on that Day] as partners"" (34:31), just as they were partners in misguidance. ""Indeed, thus do We do with the criminals"" (34:32), meaning, We do such a deed to the criminals, that is, the people of crime, who are the polytheists, as indicated by His saying, the Exalted, ""Indeed, they were, when it was said to them, 'There is no deity except Allah,' they were arrogant"" (34:33), meaning, when it was said to them, ""Say, 'There is no deity except Allah,'"" they were arrogant to accept it. The position of ""they were arrogant"" is in the accusative case as it is the predicate of ""كان"" (kaana), or in the nominative case as it is the predicate of ""إن"" (inna), and ""كان"" (kaana) is nullified. And they would say, ""Shall we leave our gods for a poet who is a madman?"" (34:34), meaning the Prophet, peace be upon him, i.e., for the saying of a poet who is a madman. So Allah, the Exalted, responded to them by saying, ""But he has come with the truth"" (34:35), meaning the Qur'an, which includes monotheism, promise, and threat. ""And he has confirmed the messengers"" (34:35), meaning, he confirmed them in what they brought of monotheism, threat, and proving the Hereafter, and he did not contradict them, nor did he bring something that the messengers before him did not bring. ""Indeed, you will taste the painful punishment"" (34:36), meaning, indeed, because of your polytheism and your disbelief, you will taste the severe, painful punishment."
8,"قال المفسرون: هذا إشارة إلى قول أبي جهل يوم بدر \"" نحن جميعٌ منتصر \"" وأصل {تَنَاصَرُونَ} تتناصرون حذفت إحدى التاءين تخفيفاً، قال تعالى {بَلْ هُمُ ٱلْيَوْمَ مُسْتَسْلِمُونَ} أي بل هم اليوم أذلاء منقادون، عاجزون عن الانتصار، سواء منهم العابدون والمعبودون {وَأَقْبَلَ بَعْضُهُمْ عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ} أي أقبل الرؤساء والأتباع يتلاومون ويتخاصمون قال أبو السعود: وسؤالهم إنما هو سؤال توبيخ بطريق الخصومة والجدال {قَالُوۤاْ إِنَّكُمْ كُنتُمْ تَأْتُونَنَا عَنِ ٱلْيَمِينِ} أي قال الأتباع منهم للمتبوعين: إِنكم كنتم تأتوننا من قبل الحقِّ، وتزينون لنا الباطل، وتصدوننا عن اتباع طريق الهدى قال الطبري: أي كنتم تأتوننا من قبل الدين والحق، فتخدعوننا بأقوى الوجوه، قال: واليمين في كلام العرب: القوة والقدرة كقول الشاعر: إِذا ما رايةٌ رفعت لمجدٍ تلقَّاها عرابةُ باليمين وقيل: المراد تأتوننا بطريق الوسوسة عن يميننا كما هو المعتاد في حالة الوسوسة بالأسرار غالباً {قَالُواْ بَلْ لَّمْ تَكُونُواْ مُؤْمِنِينَ} أي يقول لهم الرؤساء: لم نحملكم نحن على الضلال ولم نمنعكم من الإِيمان، بل كفرتم ولم تؤمنوا باختياركم قال ابن كثير: أي ليس الأمر كما تزعمون، بل كانت قلوبكم منكرةً للإِيمان، قابلةً للكفر والعصيان {وَمَا كَانَ لَنَا عَلَيْكُمْ مِّن سُلْطَانٍ} أي ما كان لنا عليكم من قوة وقدرة نقهركم بها على متابعتنا {بَلْ كُنتُمْ قَوْماً طَاغِينَ} أي بل كاف فيكم فجور وطغيان واستعداد للعصيان، فلذلك استجبتم لنا واتبعتمونا {فَحَقَّ عَلَيْنَا قَوْلُ رَبِّنَآ} أي فوجب علينا جميعاً وعيد الله لنا بالعذاب {إِنَّا لَذَآئِقُونَ} أي فإِنا لذائقو هذا العذاب لا محالة {فَأَغْوَيْنَاكُمْ إِنَّا كُنَّا غَاوِينَ} أي فزينا لكم الباطل، ودعوناكم إلى الغيّ لأننا كنا على غيٍّ وضلال، قال تعالى مخبراً عن حالهم {فَإِنَّهُمْ يَوْمَئِذٍ فِي ٱلْعَذَابِ مُشْتَرِكُونَ} أي فإِنهم يوم القيامة مشتركون في العذاب، كما كانوا مشتركين في الغواية، ولكنْ كما قال تعالى {وَلَن يَنفَعَكُمُ ٱلْيَوْمَ إِذ ظَّلَمْتُمْ أَنَّكُمْ فِي ٱلْعَذَابِ مُشْتَرِكُونَ} [الزخرف: 39] {إِنَّا كَذَلِكَ نَفْعَلُ بِٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ} أي مثل هذا الفعل بهؤلاء نفعل بالأشقياء المجرمين، ثم بيَّن تعالى السبب فقال {إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوۤاْ إِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ لاَ إِلَـٰهَ إِلاَّ ٱللَّهُ يَسْتَكْبِرُونَ} أي إِذا قيل لهم قولوا {لاَ إِلَـٰهَ إِلاَّ ٱللَّهُ} يتكبَّرون ويتعظَّمون {وَيَقُولُونَ أَءِنَّا لَتَارِكُوۤاْ آلِهَتِنَا لِشَاعِرٍ مَّجْنُونٍ}؟","The commentators said: This is a reference to Abu Jahl's statement on the day of Badr, ""We are all victorious."" The origin of {تَنَاصَرُونَ} is تتناصرون, one of the ta's has been omitted for ease. Allah said, {بَلْ هُمُ ٱلْيَوْمَ مُسْتَسْلِمُونَ} meaning, ""Rather, they are today submissive, humiliated, and unable to be victorious, whether they are worshippers or worshipped."" {وَأَقْبَلَ بَعْضُهُمْ عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ} meaning, the leaders and followers turned to each other, blaming and arguing. Abu Al-Saud said: Their questioning is a questioning of reproach through argument and debate. {قَالُوۤاْ إِنَّكُمْ كُنتُمْ تَأْتُونَنَا عَنِ ٱلْيَمِينِ} meaning, the followers said to the leaders: ""You used to come to us from the right, beautifying falsehood for us and preventing us from following the path of guidance."" Al-Tabari said: ""You used to come to us from the side of religion and truth, deceiving us in the strongest ways."" He said: ""The right in the speech of the Arabs is strength and ability, as the poet said: 'If a banner is raised for glory, it is received by 'Arubah with the right.' It has also been said: 'You come to us through whispering from our right, as is often the case with whispering secrets.' {قَالُواْ بَلْ لَّمْ تَكُونُواْ مُؤْمِنِينَ} meaning, the leaders said to them: ""We did not force you into misguidance, nor did we prevent you from faith, but you disbelieved and did not believe of your own choice."" Ibn Kathir said: ""It is not as you claim, but your hearts were rejecting faith and accepting disbelief and disobedience."" {وَمَا كَانَ لَنَا عَلَيْكُمْ مِّن سُلْطَانٍ} meaning, ""We had no power or ability over you to force you to follow us."" {بَلْ كُنتُمْ قَوْماً طَاغِينَ} meaning, ""Rather, you were a people of transgression and tyranny, and a readiness for disobedience, so you responded to us and followed us."" {فَحَقَّ عَلَيْنَا قَوْلُ رَبِّنَآ} meaning, ""The threat of our Lord to us with punishment became due upon us all."" {إِنَّا لَذَآئِقُونَ} meaning, ""We will surely taste this punishment."" {فَأَغْوَيْنَاكُمْ إِنَّا كُنَّا غَاوِينَ} meaning, ""We beautified falsehood for you and called you to misguidance because we were in misguidance and error."" Allah said, informing about their condition, {فَإِنَّهُمْ يَوْمَئِذٍ فِي ٱلْعَذَابِ مُشْتَرِكُونَ} meaning, ""On that day, they will be partners in the punishment, just as they were partners in misguidance, but as Allah said, {وَلَن يَنفَعَكُمُ ٱلْيَوْمَ إِذ ظَّلَمْتُمْ أَنَّكُمْ فِي ٱلْعَذَابِ مُشْتَرِكُونَ} [الزخرف: 39]."" {إِنَّا كَذَلِكَ نَفْعَلُ بِٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ} meaning, ""We do this to the criminals in this way."" Then Allah explained the reason, saying, {إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوۤاْ إِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ لاَ إِلَـٰهَ إِلاَّ ٱللَّهُ يَسْتَكْبِرُونَ} meaning, ""When they were told, 'Say, There is no deity except Allah,' they would be arrogant and proud."" {وَيَقُولُونَ أَءِنَّا لَتَارِكُوۤاْ آلِهَتِنَا لِشَاعِرٍ مَّجْنُونٍ}?"
9,"يعنون بذلك رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم قال تعالى رداً عليهم {بَلْ جَآءَ بِٱلْحَقِّ وَصَدَّقَ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ} أي ليس الأمرَ كما يفترون بل جاءهم محمد بالتوحيد والإِسلام الذي هو الحقُّ الأبلج، وجاء بمثل ما جاء به الرسل قبله قال أبو حيان: جمع المشركون بين إِنكار الوحدانية، وإِنكار الرسالة، ثم خلطوا في كلامهم بقولهم \"" شاعر مجنون \"" فإِن الشاعر عنده من الفهم والحذق ما ينظم به المعاني الغريبة، ويصوغها في قالب الألفاظ البديعة، ومن كان مجنوناً لا يصل إلى شيء من ذلك، فكلامهم تخليط وهذيان {إِنَّكُمْ لَذَآئِقُو ٱلْعَذَابِ ٱلأَلِيمِ} أي إنكم أيها المجرمون لمعذبون أشد العذاب {وَمَا تُجْزَوْنَ إِلاَّ مَا كُنْتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ} أي لا تُعاقبون إلا جزاء مثل عملكم قال الصاوي: لأن الشر يكون جزاؤه بقدره، بخلاف الخير فجزاؤه بأضعاف مضاعفة … ولمّا ذكر شيئاً من أحوال الكفار وعذابهم، ذكر شيئاً من أحوال المؤمنين ونعيمهم، على طريقة القرآن في الموازنة بين الفريقين ترغيباً وترهيباً فقال {إِلاَّ عِبَادَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلْمُخْلَصِينَ} الاستثناء منقطع أي لكنْ عباد الله المُخلَصين الموحدين، فإِنهم لا يذوقون العذاب، ولا يناقشون الحساب، بل يتجاوز الله عن سيئاتهم، يُجزون الحسنة بعشر أمثالها إلى سبعمائة ضعف..","They mean by that the Messenger of Allah, may Allah's prayers and peace be upon him. Allah the Exalted said in response to them: {But he has come with the truth and confirmed the messengers} [34:43], meaning, it is not as they falsely claim, but Muhammad came to them with monotheism and Islam, which is the clear truth, and he came with what the messengers before him had brought. Abu Hayyan said: The polytheists combined the denial of oneness and the denial of prophethood, and then mixed in their speech by saying, ""a poet, a madman."" For the poet has understanding and skill to organize strange meanings and shape them in the form of wonderful words, and whoever is mad cannot achieve any of that. So their speech is confusion and delirium. {Indeed, you will taste the painful punishment} [34:42], meaning, O you criminals, you will be punished with the most severe punishment. {And you will not be recompensed except for what you used to do} [34:51], meaning, you will not be punished except with a recompense like your deeds. As-Sawi said: Because evil is recompensed according to its measure, unlike good, for its recompense is multiplied many times... And when He mentioned something of the state of the disbelievers and their punishment, He mentioned something of the state of the believers and their bliss, following the Quran's method of balancing between the two groups, encouraging and warning. He said: {Except the servants of Allah, the chosen ones} [34:40], an interrupted exception, meaning, but the servants of Allah, the chosen ones, the monotheists, for they will not taste punishment, nor will they be questioned about their accounts, but Allah will overlook their sins, and they will be rewarded with ten times the good deed up to seven hundred times."
10,ثم أخبر عن جزائهم فقال {أُوْلَئِكَ لَهُمْ رِزْقٌ مَّعْلُومٌ} أي أولئك الأخيار الأبرار لهم رزقهم في الجنة صباحاً ومساءً كما قال تعالى {وَلَهُمْ رِزْقُهُمْ فِيهَا بُكْرَةً وَعَشِيّاً} [مريم: 62] وقال بهم كيف شاءوا قال مجاهد: {مُّتَقَابِلِينَ} أي لا ينظر بعضُهم إلى قفا بعض تواصلاً وتحابباً {يُطَافُ عَلَيْهِمْ بِكَأْسٍ مِّن مَّعِينٍ} لما ذكر الطعام أعقبه بذكر الشراب أي يطوف عليهم خدم الجنة بكأسٍ من الخمر من نهر جارٍ خارج من عيون الجنة قال الصاوي: وصف به خمر الجنة لأنه يجري كالماء النابع وقال ابن عباس: كل كأسٍ في القرآن فهي الخمر، والمعين هي الجارية {بَيْضَآءَ لَذَّةٍ لِّلشَّارِبِينَ} أي هذه الخمر بيضاء ذات لذةً للشاربين، يلتذ بها من شربها قال الحسن: خمر الجنة أشد بياضاً من اللبن {لاَ فِيهَا غَوْلٌ وَلاَ هُمْ عَنْهَا يُنزَفُونَ} أي ليس فيها ما يغتال عقولهم فيفسدها، ولا هم يسكرون بشربها كما تفعل خمر الدنيا قال ابن كثير: نزَّه الله سبحانه خمر الجنة عن الآفات التي هي في خمر الدنيا، من صداع الرأس، ووجع البطن، وذهاب العقل، فخمر الجنة طعمها طيب كلونها، والمراد بالغول هنا صُداع الرأس قاله ابن عباس، وقال قتادة: هو صداع الرأس ووجع البطن وتلك أجمل أوصاف الشراب، التي تحقق لذة الشُّرَّاب، وتنفي أكداره وأضراره، فلا خُمار يصدع الرءوس، ولا سكر ولا عربدة يُذهب لذة الاستمتاع كما هي الحال في خمرة الدنيا {وَعِندَهُمْ قَاصِرَاتُ ٱلطَّرْفِ} أي وعندهم الحور العين، العفيفات اللواتي قصرن أعينهن على النظر إلى أزواجهن، فلا ينظرن إلى غيرهم حياءً وعفةً، قال ابن عباس: {قَاصِرَاتُ ٱلطَّرْفِ} أي عفيفات لا ينظرن إلى غير أزواجهن {عِينٌ} أي وهنَّ مع العفة واسعات جميلات العيون قال الطبري: أي نُجل العيون جمع عيناء وهي المرأة الواسعة العين مع الحسن والجمال، وهي أحسن ما تكون من العيون {كَأَنَّهُنَّ بَيْضٌ مَّكْنُونٌ} أي كأنهن اللؤلؤ المكنون في أصدافه قاله ابن عباس واستشهد بقوله تعالى {وَحُورٌ عِينٌ * كَأَمْثَالِ ٱللُّؤْلُؤِ ٱلْمَكْنُونِ} [الواقعة: 22 - 23] وقال الحسن: {ٱلْمَكْنُونِ} المصون الذي لم تمسَّه الأيدي … والغرضُ أنهنَّ مع هذا الجمال الباهر، مصونات كالدُّر في أصدافه، مع رقةٍ ولطفٍ ونعومة {كَأَنَّهُنَّ بَيْضٌ مَّكْنُونٌ} لا تبتذله الأيدي ولا العيون، والعربُ تشبّه المرأة بالبيضة لصفائها وبياضها قال أبو حيان: ذكر تعالى في هذه الآيات أولاً الرزق وهو ما تتلذذ به الأجسام، وثانياً الإِكرام وهو ما تتلذذ به النفوس، ثم ذكر المحل وهو جنات النعيم، ثم لذة التآنس والاجتماع {عَلَىٰ سُرُرٍ مُّتَقَابِلِينَ} وهو أتم للسرور وآنس، ثم ذكر المشروب وهو الخمر التي تدار عليهم بالكؤوس ولا يتناولونها بأنفسهم، ثم ختم باللذة الجسدية - أبلغ الملاذ - وهي التآنس بالنساء ثم أخبر تعالى عما يتحدث به أهل الجنة للأنس والسرور، وهم على موائد الشراب يتلذذون بكل ممتع، وينعمون بتجاذب أطراف الحديث فقال {فَأَقْبَلَ بَعْضُهُمْ عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ} أي جلسوا يتحدثون عما جرى لهم في الدنيا، يتذاكرون نعيمهم وحال الدنيا وثمرة الإِيمان {قَالَ قَآئِلٌ مِّنْهُمْ إِنِّي كَانَ لِي قَرِينٌ} أي قال قائل من أهل الجنة إني كان لي في الدنيا صديقٌ وجليس ينكر البعث {يَقُولُ أَءِنَّكَ لَمِنَ ٱلْمُصَدِّقِينَ} أي يقول لي أتصدِّق بالبعث والجزاء؟,"Then he spoke of their reward and said, ""Those are the ones for whom there is a known provision."" That is, those righteous people have their provision in Paradise in the morning and evening, as Allah, the Most High, said, ""And they will have therein their provision, morning and evening."" [Maryam: 62] Abu Al-Saud said, ""Known for its characteristics of good appearance, delicious taste, and pleasant smell."" Then he interpreted the provision by saying, ""Fruits, and they will be honored."" That is, various fruits of all that they desire, and they will be honored and respected in Paradise. He mentioned fruits specifically because everything that is eaten in Paradise is for enjoyment and pleasure. ""In the Gardens of Bliss,"" that is, in gardens and orchards where they enjoy themselves. ""On couches facing each other,"" that is, on thrones adorned with pearls and rubies, turning around as they wish. Mujahid said, ""Facing each other,"" meaning that they do not look at each other's backs, out of affection and love. ""They will be served with a cup of flowing wine,"" when he mentioned food, he followed it with mentioning drink, meaning that the servants of Paradise will serve them with a cup of wine from a flowing river, coming out of the springs of Paradise. Al-Sawi said, ""It is described as such because it flows like spring water."" Ibn Abbas said, ""Every cup in the Quran is wine, and flowing means running."" ""White, a delight to the drinkers,"" meaning this wine is white and a delight to the drinkers, enjoyed by whoever drinks it. Al-Hasan said, ""The wine of Paradise is whiter than milk."" ""In it, there is no harm, nor are they made to stagger,"" meaning it does not harm their minds and corrupt them, nor do they get drunk from drinking it, as is the case with the wine of this world. Ibn Kathir said, ""Allah, the Most High, has purified the wine of Paradise from the afflictions that are in the wine of this world, such as headache, stomach ache, and loss of reason. The wine of Paradise is as pleasant to taste as it is to look at. The intended meaning of harm here is headache, as said by Ibn Abbas. Qatadah said, it is headache and stomach ache. These are the best descriptions of a drink, which achieve the pleasure of the drinkers and negate its impurities and harms. There is no hangover to cause headaches, no drunkenness, nor any disorder that would spoil the enjoyment, as is the case with the wine of this world. ""And with them are those who restrain their glances,"" meaning with them are the Houris, the chaste ones who restrain their glances to look only at their husbands, not looking at others out of modesty and chastity. Ibn Abbas said, ""Those who restrain their glances,"" meaning chaste, not looking at others than their husbands. ""Wide-eyed,"" meaning they are chaste and beautiful-eyed. Al-Tabari said, ""Wide-eyed,"" meaning large-eyed, a collection of 'aina, which is a woman with large eyes and beauty, the most beautiful of eyes. ""As if they were hidden pearls,"" meaning as if they were pearls hidden in their shells, as said by Ibn Abbas, and he cited the saying of Allah, the Most High, ""And fair women with wide, [beautiful] eyes, as if they were [beautiful] pearls [that come out of their shells]."" [Al-Waqi'ah: 22-23] Al-Hasan said, ""Hidden,"" meaning protected, not touched by hands. The intent is that they, with this radiant beauty, are protected like pearls in their shells, with gentleness, softness, and smoothness. ""As if they were hidden pearls,"" not handled by hands or eyes. The Arabs liken a woman to an egg because of its clarity and whiteness. Abu Hayyan said, ""Allah, the Most High, mentioned in these verses first the provision, which is what the bodies delight in, and second, honor, which is what the souls delight in. Then He mentioned the place, which is the Gardens of Bliss, then the pleasure of companionship and gathering, 'On couches facing each other,' which is more perfect for joy and companionship. Then He mentioned the drink, which is wine served to them in cups, and they do not take it themselves. Then He concluded with physical pleasure, the ultimate of pleasures, which is companionship with women. Then Allah, the Most High, spoke of what the people of Paradise talk about for pleasure and joy, sitting at the tables of drink, enjoying every delight, and delighting in conversing, saying, 'Then they will turn to one another, questioning,' meaning they will sit and talk about what happened to them in this world, remembering their bliss and the state of this world and the fruit of faith. 'A speaker among them will say, 'Indeed, I had a companion,' meaning a speaker from the people of Paradise will say, 'I had a friend and companion in this world who denied the Resurrection.' 'He would say, 'Do you believe in the Resurrection and the Recompense?'"""
11,{فاستفتهم} أي استخبرهم {أهم أشد خلقاً}، قيل: أراد كفار مكة، وقيل: نزلت في أيام الأسد بن كلدة، قيل: هذا سؤال تقرير، وقيل: سؤال توبيخ {أمّن خلقنا} يريد ما ذكر من خلائقه من الملائكة والسماوات والأرض والمشارق والكواكب والشهب الثاقب والشياطين، وقيل: أراد الأمم الماضية، وقيل: أهلكناهم بذنوبهم {إنَّا خلقناهم من طين لازب} أي لازم يعني آدم (عليه السلام) {بل عجبت} من قدرة الله على هذه الخلائق العظيمة وهم يسخرون منك ومن تعجبك، أو من إنكارهم البعث وهم يسخرون من أمر البعث {وإذا ذكروا لا يذكرون} إذا وعظوا لا يتعظون {وإذا رأوا آية} من آيات الله كانشقاق القمر وغيره {يستسخرون} يبالغون في السخريَّة {وقالوا إن هذا} القرآن وسائر ما يذكره الرسول {إلا سحر مبين} {أئذا متنا وكنا تراباً وعظاماً أئنا لمبعوثون} {أو آباؤنا الأولون} أي كيف يبعث آباؤنا بعد أن صاروا تراباً {قل نعم وأنتم داخرون} صاغرون، والمعنى تبعثون وأنتم داخرون صاغرون {فإنما هي} جواب شرط مقدر تقديره إذا كان ذلك فما هي إلا {زجرة واحدة} وهي النفخة الثانية، والزجرة الصيحة من قولك زجر الراعي الابل والغنم إذا صاح عليها {فإذا هم ينظرون} إحياء، وقيل: ينظرون إلى ما نزل بهم، وقيل: ينظر بعضهم إلى بعض: {وقالوا يا ويلنا} يدعون بالويل لما عاينوا من العذاب {هذا يوم الدين} يوم الجزاء والحساب، هذا من كلام الكفرة بعضهم لبعض إلى قوله: {احشروا الذين} أو يكون من كلام الملائكة بعضهم لبعض {هذا يوم الفصل} أي يوم يفصل الله بين عباده {الذي كنتم به تكذبون} يعني يكذبون الرسل {احشروا الذين ظلموا} الناس أو ظلموا الرسل بالتكذيب {وأزواجهم} أشباههم، وقيل: أتباعهم، وقيل: أزواجهم المشركات {وما كانوا يعبدون} {من دون الله} في الدنيا مثل الأصنام، وقيل: الشياطين الذين أطاعوهم {فاهدوهم}، قيل: ادعوهم، وقيل: دلوهم {إلى صراط الجحيم}، قيل: طريق النار {وقفوهم إنهم مسؤولون}، قيل: هذه مساءلة توبيخ، وقيل: عن قول لا إلا إلاَّ الله، وقيل: عن خطاياهم، وقيل: عن جميع أحوالهم وأفعالهم {ما لكم لا تناصرون} يقال لهم توبيخا: ما لكم لا ينصر بعضكم بعضاً في دفع العذاب؟,"""Then ask them,"" meaning inquire of them, ""Are they stronger in creation?"" It is said that he meant the disbelievers of Mecca, and it is said that it was revealed during the days of Al-Asad ibn Kulda. It is said that this is a question of affirmation, and it is said that it is a question of reproach. ""Or what We have created,"" meaning what We have mentioned of Our creations, such as the angels, the heavens, the earth, the easts, the planets, the piercing meteor, and the devils. It is said that he meant the past nations, and it is said that We destroyed them for their sins. ""Indeed, We created them from sticky clay,"" meaning adhering, referring to Adam (peace be upon him). ""But they are amazed,"" at the power of Allah over these great creations, and they mock you and your amazement, or at their denial of the resurrection while they mock the matter of the resurrection. ""And when they are reminded, they do not remember,"" when they are admonished, they do not take heed. ""And when they see a sign,"" from the signs of Allah, such as the splitting of the moon and other things, ""they ridicule,"" they exaggerate in mockery. ""And they said, 'This,'"" meaning the Quran and all that the Messenger mentions, ""is not but clear magic."" ""When we have died and become dust and bones, will we indeed be resurrected?"" ""Or our forefathers of old?"" meaning how will our forefathers be resurrected after they have become dust? ""Say, 'Yes, and you will be humiliated.'"" meaning you will be resurrected and you will be humiliated. ""Then it will be only a single shout,"" meaning the second blast, and the shout is a cry, from your saying, 'The shepherd cried out to the camels and sheep.' ""Then behold, they will be looking,"" meaning they will be revived, and it is said that they will be looking at what befell them, and it is said that they will be looking at one another. ""And they will say, 'O woe to us!'"" they will call for woe when they see the punishment. ""This is the Day of Recompense,"" the Day of retribution and reckoning, this is from the speech of the disbelievers to one another, up to His saying: ""Gather those who did wrong,"" or it may be from the speech of the angels to one another. ""This is the Day of Judgment,"" meaning the Day when Allah will judge between His servants. ""The one which you used to deny,"" meaning they denied the messengers. ""Gather those who wronged,"" the people, or those who wronged the messengers by denying them. ""And their companions,"" their likenesses, and it is said their followers, and it is said their polytheistic wives. ""And what they used to worship,"" ""besides Allah,"" in the world, such as idols, and it is said the devils whom they obeyed. ""Then lead them,"" it is said, call them, and it is said, guide them. ""To the path of Hell,"" it is said, the path to the Fire. ""And stop them; indeed, they are to be questioned,"" it is said, this is a questioning of reproach, and it is said, about the saying, 'There is no god except Allah,' and it is said, about their sins, and it is said, about all their states and deeds. ""What is [the matter] with you that you do not aid each other?"" it is said to them in reproach: Why does not some of you aid others in warding off the punishment?"
